---

## Data We Collect

**KenyanLiveTV** does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

However, the app uses **Google Firebase Analytics**, a third-party service, which automatically collects the following:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- App interactions and usage patterns (screens viewed, buttons tapped)
- App performance data (crash reports, diagnostics)
- IP address and mobile device information

This data is collected anonymously for the purpose of improving the app experience and is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## How We Use Data

Data collected through Firebase Analytics is used solely for:

- Monitoring and improving app performance
- Understanding how users interact with KenyanLiveTV
- Diagnosing and fixing crashes

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third party beyond what Google Firebase requires to operate.

---

## Local Storage

The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to any server:

- Your favorite TV channels and radio stations
- Your recently played channels and stations
- Sleep timer preferences

---

## Children's Privacy

KenyanLiveTV does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through this app, please contact us at **ndemorich@gmail.com** and we will take steps to remove that information.
